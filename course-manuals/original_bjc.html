This course was made to mimic the moodle-based BJC course as it existed in the academic year 2012-2013. To get as close an approximation, you will need to find the SVN tag 'original_bjc', as the files will go modification after that version. There are some differences, even at that tag: - we have no forum tool in the repository, at least when this course was made. - some migration from BYOB to snap probably was done too quickly. - some of the images don't work, etc.
